## Financial Logic of the Seed
*   **Total Positive Profit:** The organization as a whole is profitable because most products (7 out of 10) have a high sales volume.
*   **Loss per Item:** The last 3 products of each list have a very low sales volume (we bought 150 units in total and sold only ~9). In the Dashboard, this will appear as a "loss" or negative margin for these specific items in the analyzed period, because the inventory cost (purchases) greatly exceeded the revenue.
*   **Relationship:** All profit logic is based on the volume of `SalesOrder` vs `PurchaseOrder`.
